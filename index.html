What is Hacktoberfest?

Hacktoberfest is a hackathon with a tip of the hat to fall, Oktoberfest, and Bavarian flare.

If you work in interface design or graphic design, or you currently build websites, apps, and web services, you are probably a good fit!

Come in with an idea...

  • a website design or idea
  • a SaaS idea
  • an open source project
  • a civic project
  • a game idea
  • an experimental graphics library
  • and so forth...

Then start building it!

  • bring in a team to work on the idea
  • meet other hackers in Houston
  • or fly solo
  • hold a quick agile workshop
  • put together a quick kanban board
  • research your tools / APIs
  • polish a design
  • start the coding process

All we ask is that you enjoy beer, bratwurst, giant sourdough pretzels, sauerkraut, lederhosen, yodelling, the sound of music, BMW's, Jägermeister, code, polka music, design, or some combination of them. :-)

Questions & Answers

If you've got questions, we happily have answers! Email us at houston@theironyard.com.

I RSVP'd. What should I bring?

Bring your laptop (that you can program/design on), and if you like, your favorite lederhosen!

Must I be 21?

No, but if you are not 21, then you must be accompanied by a parent or legal guardian.

Can I BYOB?

No outside alcoholic beverages may be brought onto premises or the parking area. Sorry, flask connoisseurs.
